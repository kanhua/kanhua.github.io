About Me

  • Lead internal R&D projects

Professional interests

My primary research focus is on computer vision, with a particular interest in areas intersecting with physics. Recently, I have concentrated on:

  • Human Action Recognition and Anticipation: Developing methods to analyze and predict human actions in video sequences
  • Multi-View Geometry & 3D Reconstruction: Advancing techniques for reconstructing three-dimensional models from multi-view imagery

Before I transitioned to the ML/AI field, I was an experimental physicist with primarily focuses in:

  • Semiconductor and optical physics with their applications in solar energy
  • Numerical Modeling
  • Novel semiconductor characterization techniques
