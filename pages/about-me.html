About Me

I am a semiconductor physicist specialising in III-V compound semiconductor solar cells. My main research interest is the applications of nanotechnologies and other advanced design concepts for industry-quality solar cells. My experience covers many aspects of solar cell research, particularly in solar cell design and characterisation. I am skilled in building electro-optics instrument, writing native Windows GUI control softwares, and writing scientific scripts with various programming languages.

Apart from my professional life, I enjoy learning new skillls through MOOC. Currently I am working towards the completion of a Nanodegree in Data Analyst with Udacity.
